Here's a timeline of important events for the Duchess of Cambridge

  • 1982:Catherine Elizabeth Middleton is born at Royal Berkshire Hospital.
  • 1984:Catherine and her family moved to Amman in Jordan with her family. She began attending nursery school when she was three years old.
  • 1986:Catherine and her family move back to West Berkshire in England. She starts to attend St. Andrew's School near Pangbourne.
  • 1995:Catherine leaves St. Andrew's School. She attends Marlborough College in Wiltshire where she did well in her classes and participated in a variety of sports.
  • 2000:Catherine finishes school at Marlborough College. She took a gap year by participating in various activities around the world.
  • 2001:Catherine enrolls at the University of St. Andrews in Scotland studying Art History. During this time she met Prince William, who is also attending St. Andrews.
  • 2005:Catherine graduates from St. Andrews. Following graduation, she begins to assist her parents with their company Party Pieces.
  • 2006:Catherine begins working at Jigsaw Junior as a part time buyer.
  • 2007:Catherine leaves Jigsaw Junior. She briefly splits up from Prince William, but the two reconcil a short time later.
  • 2008:Catherine helps her parents launch First Birthdays, a junior brand of Party Pieces.
  • 2010:Catherine and Prince William become engaged while in Kenya. They formally announce their engagement to the public a month later. William gives Catherien his late mother's engagement ring.
  • 2011:Catherine leaves Party Pieces. She begins carrying out her first public engagements alongside Prince William before the royal wedding. The two marry April 29 at Westminister Abbey. Following the wedding, Catherine accompanies her husband on their first royal tour to Canada.
  • 2012:Catherine and William complete their second royal tour to Singapore, Malaysia, Tuvalu, and Solomon Islands. By the end of the year, Clarence House announces Catherine is pregnant with the couple's first child.
  • 2013:Catherine and William welcome Prince George at St. Mary's Hospital in London.
  • 2014:Catherine goes on her third royal tour to Australia with William and George. In September, Clarence House announces Catherine's second pregnancy. The couple did a short tour in New York at the beginning of December.
  • 2015:Catherine and William welcome Princess Charlotte at St. Mary's Hospital in London.
  • 2016:Catherine is a guest editor for Huffington Post UK in February to launch Young Minds Matter initiative. She and William go on their fourth royal tour to India and Bhutan in April.
  • 2017:Catherine and her family go on a fifth royal tour to Poland and Germany in July.
